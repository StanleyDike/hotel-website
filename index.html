About Our Hotel

The History Of Our Hotel

The Marriott Hotel chain was founded by J. Willard Marriott in 1927, originally as a root beer stand in Washington D.C. It expanded over the years to include hotels and resorts in various locations around the world. In 1964, the company went public and by the 1970s it had become one of the largest hotel chains in the United States.
